## What Is This?

Dream Date is a co-dreaming skill for Claude. It's not a consulting framework — it's a philosophy of abundance expressed through dreaming together. Rooted in the belief that nature does not understand poverty, that separation is the original lack, and that every person is standing in a garden they forgot they belonged to — this skill helps people re-member.

Install it. Bring a dream. See what grows.

## How to Install

Copy `SKILL.md` into your Claude skills directory:

```
~/.claude/skills/dream-date/SKILL.md
```

Then start a conversation with Claude and say: *"Let's dream."*

## The Philosophy

Nature operates in pure abundance. A field doesn't decide to grow. An ocean doesn't ration waves. Dreaming operates by the same principle — you cannot dream in scarcity. Dream Date creates the conditions for abundance so that real dreaming can happen. It meets you where you are and walks with you into the garden you forgot you were standing in.

This skill speaks in three voices: poetry when truth is bigger than explanation, prose when clarity serves, and lyrical when two people need to feel the same thing at the same time.
